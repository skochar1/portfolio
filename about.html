About Me

Thanks for stopping by to read about me. I am a motivated undergraduate student studying Computer Science at wellesley College. I love writing, reading, and fighting for what I believe in.

Shreya Unraveled

I was born in Fremont, CA in 2001. I've lived in California my entire life, so coming to Massachusetts to study was a big change for me! In college, I've mainly focused on growing my network and career as a woman in STEM. I have run the Wellesley College Girls Who Code chapter since 2020. Teaching people how to code has slowly turned into one of my biggest passions, and in December 2020, my friend (Aneesha Sreerama) and I started the Python Research Program. We created this as a means for high school students to gain insight into the ever-growing fields of technology.
