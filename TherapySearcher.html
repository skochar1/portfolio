Therapy Searcher App

A widespread problem for students has been finding a proper mental health professional for their needs. School mental health resources often do not provide enough support, and are overburdened by the amount of students. Several students are deterred from even attempting to go to therapy because of the amount of time it takes to find a therapist that fit their needs (ex. monetary issues, insurance accepted or not, availability to accept new clients, specialties on specific mental health issues, figuring out if they are a “fit”). We have designed an app that helps users find mental health professionals that meet their needs quickly.

My contributions: prototyping (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap) and sketching
